The Centenario · MMXVI

Twenty coupés, twenty roadsters — a century of Ferruccio's vision, sealed in carbon.

Anatomy of an Icon

Three obsessions, one object.

Nothing on the Centenario is incidental. Follow the three decisions that shaped it — the visual holds while the story moves.

01 / The Heart

A V12 that answers to nobody.

6.5 litres, naturally aspirated, spinning to 8,500 rpm for 770 CV. No turbos, no electric crutch — just twelve cylinders and the sound they make.

02 / The Frame

Carbon, from tub to tail.

A full carbon-fibre monocoque holds dry weight to 1,520 kg. The result is a power-to-weight ratio that treats physics as a suggestion.

03 / The Wind

Shaped to disappear into air.

Active aerodynamics and rear-wheel steering make 350+ km/h feel deliberate. Every surface earns its place in the airflow.
